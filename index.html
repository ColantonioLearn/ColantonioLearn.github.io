Hello! I'm Joseph Colantonio.

I'm a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Harvard in the Graduate School of Education. I'm currently affiliated with the Computational Cognitve Development (Education) Lab, spearheaded by Elizabeth Bonawitz.
My current research interests combine computational approaches and experimental design to investigate the intersections of the motivational, emotional, and (neuro-)physiological mechanisms of learning. I also do look at the relations among play, exploration, and social interactions.
